Rock Paper or Scissors ??

Instructions on how to play this game:

  1. You have an option to choose three of the following options
    • Rock
    • Paper
    • Scissors
  2. The Way it works Paper beats Rock, Rock beats Scissors, and Scissors beats Paper.
  3. When you choose your answer/choice the computer randomly loads an answer and the two of you battle.

So..Rock Paper or Scissors
